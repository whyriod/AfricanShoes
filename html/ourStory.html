The Need:

Africa contains most of the poorest countries in the world. Thousands of Children wonder where the next meal will come from, let alone what they will wear. Marauding Warlords often steal anything of value, and so the people are left to suffer.

John Smith, our Founding Father, established Bargain Shoe Sales in 1997 after his mission for “The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints”. He is fluent in 12 languages, mostly regional dialects of various tribes in Africa. Besides this outreach, John is the CEO of 12 different companies.
