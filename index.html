Bargin Shoe Sales!

Welcome to Bargain Shoe Sales! We are a company committed to delivering you quality shoes at a bargain price. While we believe our customers are of the utmost importance, we also realize that many in this world do not have many of the blessings that we enjoy in developed countries. Therefore, Bargain Shoe Sales is committed to taking donated lightly-used shoes and sending them to African families that are suffering in poverty. With each purchase of a bargain shoe, you are enabling us to send two pairs of shoes to children in the following countries:

  1. Burundi,
  2. Central African Republic
  3. Democratic Republic of the Congo
  4. Eritrea
  5. Etc.

We hope you will enjoy our quality shoes, but we hope even more that you will feel peace knowing your money is going to a good place.
